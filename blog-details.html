Quiz games are interactive games where players are presented with questions and challenges that they must answer or solve. These questions can cover a wide range of topics such as general knowledge, trivia, history, science, sports, entertainment, and more. Players typically select answers from multiple-choice options or input their responses directly.

Quiz games can be played individually or in multiplayer formats, where players compete against each other to see who can answer the most questions correctly or achieve the highest score. They are available in various formats, including mobile apps, online platforms, television shows, board games, and even in-person events.

The primary objective of quiz games is to entertain, educate, and engage players by testing their knowledge and critical thinking skills in a fun and interactive way. They are popular among people of all ages and are often used as a tool for learning, socializing, and friendly competition.
